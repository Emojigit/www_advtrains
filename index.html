Advanced Trains

Advanced Trains (advtrains) is a Mod for the Minetest engine. It adds trains and various equipment for building and operating railways in Minetest worlds.

Minetest Forum Page

Advanced Trains have its Minetest Forum Page. Important announcements are made on it.

Servers

There are some servers running the Advanced Trains mod:

  • Linux Forks
  • Grand Theft Auto Minetest
  • Pandorabox
  • ITALIA ULLA SERVER
  • Crimea HiTech&Magic (RUS)
  • Survivalworks Urban Edition
  • C&C Minetest Servers

There might be more of which I am not aware.
